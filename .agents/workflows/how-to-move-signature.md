# ✍️ How to Move and Edit Signatures

Follow these steps to professionally position and sign your documents:

### 1. Instant Grab & Move
- **Action**: Click and hold your mouse **anywhere** inside the signature box.
- **Why**: The entire box is now a "Live Zone." You don't have to aim for the border or the handle anymore—just reach out and grab it!
- **Result**: The box will follow your hand perfectly, even if you are zoomed in.

### 2. Positioning
- **Action**: Drag the box to your desired location on any page. 
- **Dropping**: Let go of the mouse to "drop" it into place. The editor will automatically save the new position.

### 3. Signing (The "Manual Action" Rule)
To prevent the signature window from popping up while you're trying to move, I've made signing a deliberate action:
- **Option A (Button)**: Click the box once to select it, then click the indigo **"SIGN NOW"** button that appears right above it.
- **Option B (Double-Click)**: Rapidly double-click anywhere on the signature box to jump straight into the signature window.

### 4. Resizing
- **Action**: Use the white circular handles on the corners or the square handles on the sides.
- **Tip**: Corner handles will maintain the signature's professional proportions.

### 5. Deleting
- **Action**: Select the box and click the red **Trash icon** that appears in the top-right corner.
